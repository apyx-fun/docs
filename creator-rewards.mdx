## How it works

When someone **buys** your token on the bonding curve, a **creator fee** is taken from the buy (in SOL) and sent to your **creator vault** — the wallet you set when you launched the token.

- The fee is a **percentage of the buy** (set in basis points by the protocol, e.g. 1% = 100 bps).
- It is **only on buys**. Sells do not pay a creator fee.
- The fee is transferred **on-chain** with every buy; you don’t need to claim. It goes straight to your vault.

So: the more volume on your token’s curve, the more SOL you earn as the creator. This aligns creator incentives with making a token people want to buy and hold.
