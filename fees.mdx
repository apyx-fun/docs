## Fee overview

| Fee | Rate |
|-----|------|
| **Trade creator fees** | 0.5% |
| **Trade protocol fees** | 0% |
| **Duel resolution fees** | 5% of the loser’s SOL reserves |
| **Migration to PumpSwap** | 6 SOL fixed |

---

## Trade creator fees — 0.5%

When someone **buys** your token on the bonding curve, **0.5% of the trade** is taken as a creator fee and sent (in SOL) to your creator vault. Sells do not pay a creator fee. You can claim from your profile page when logged in with the wallet you used to launch the token.

---

## Trade protocol fees — 0%

There is **no protocol fee** on buys or sells. Only the creator fee applies on buys.

---

## Duel resolution fees — 5%

When a duel resolves, **5% of the loser’s SOL reserves** (the SOL in the loser token’s bonding curve) is taken as a protocol fee. The rest of the loser’s SOL is moved to the winner’s curve and used to buy winner tokens for the mercy vault. So the loser’s curve is drained; 5% goes to the protocol, the remainder funds the winner and mercy.

---

## Migration to PumpSwap — 6 SOL fixed

When a token **graduates** (bonding curve sells out), a **fixed 6 SOL** graduation fee is paid from the curve’s SOL to the protocol before the rest is used to create the PumpSwap pool. So the pool is funded with (curve SOL − 6 SOL).
